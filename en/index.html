DRAFTLAB

Combining years of experience, we were born to transform needs or opportunities in digital cross-media experience, focusing on real audience engagement, developing results unimaginable before.

Our goal is to be the arm of technology and innovation marketing teams and agencies, in order to enrich the brainstorm and put into practice ideas and actions barred by the complexity and lack of resources.

We assist our partners in developing mobile, interactive actions with kinect, arduino and processing. We use technologies PHP, Python and Ruby to deliver a quality project and agile.

We are a digital projects producer, focused on deliver solutions in a simple, fast and easy way.

WHAT DO WE DELIVER?

  • MOBILE TECHNOLOGY

    We deliver mobile projects using the main technologies available, through tools as PhoneGAP and Titanium or even native developing as Objective C.

  • PHYSICAL INTERACTIONS

    We connect a digital action to real world, allowing an engagement action by creating a "like" on Facebook or capturing people movements.

  • AGILE DEVELOPMENT

    Work on agile development methods such as Scrum and XP to deliver high quality projects in expected due date.

  • WEB PROJECTS

    We work with PHP, Python and Ruby implementing projects such as: corporate portals, landing pages, e-commerce and hotsites, allowing an web action focused on results.

  • TOUCH APPLICATIONS

    We develop applications focused on touch platforms such as TV, showcases, floors and tablets.

  • GAMES

    With SDKs support for games, we produce advergames for web, smartphones and tablets.

PARTNERS

We are proud to work with partners that are looking for ways to engage and stimulate public through fun and interactive actions.
